About Us

The company " Rolometal " was founded in 1999 in Becej. Today, the company is engaged in the production, sale and installation of various products, such as:

  • PVC and aluminum joinery
  • PVC and aluminum shutters
  • Drips
  • Window sills

Roller shutters and fixed mosquito nets and venetian blinds are available. In addition to the mentioned jobs, Rolometal also transports goods in international and domestic traffic.
The success and the achieved results are due to both the staff in the commercial and the workers in production and transport.
The company's plans for the future are to increase the volume of production with the help of modern work technology, increase the number of employees and expand the market, both in our country and abroad.

For the quality of work, Rolometal also received the decoration of the Novi Sad Fair in 2000.

Products

Profile systems are of internationally recognized quality, reliability, unique design. They satisfy even the strictest technical requirements of the profession, but also the requirements of end users. So, they enable high quality products that increase the value of your home.

PVC carpentry

We make products from very resistant PVC profiles:

  • Deceuninck / Thysen
  • Decco
Profiles

The profile systems contain 5 and 6 chambers, which primarily ensure the stability of the frames and in addition provide greater air insulation.
We install glazed windows with thermopane glass, filled with argon on request (gas that provides better thermal insulation).

  • The frames are chained with internal galvanized steel reinforcement.
  • The profiles are in accordance with the European standard EN-12 608, classified in the highest "A" category with the thickness of the outer walls up to 3 mm.
  • Two or three rubber seals protect the fitting from moisture and dust, extending its service life
  • Outstanding thermal and sound insulation
  • Above-average waterproofing
  • Increased safety, the backrest of the middle seal prevents the attempt to raise the sash
  • Installation depth 72mm to 78mm
  • Steel supported on a length of 4cm guarantees excellent static characteristics
  • Glass grip up to 42mm thick
  • Low silhouette of the stem and sash provides a large bright window area
  • External profile line designed to allow efficient drainage of atmospheric precipitation
  • Colored profiles
  • Glass: the possibility of installing all types of glass

We also produce sliding doors, smaller garage doors, external accordion doors, shop windows (portals), and other products made of PVC material, at the request of customers.

  • The carpentry is equipped with Siegenia-Aubi or Vabis fittings.
  • The lock is in five points (Multi-lock).
  • We give a guarantee of 2 to 10 years for these products.
